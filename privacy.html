Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026 · Last updated: March 4, 2026

AVEOL ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in plain language.

1. Who We Are

AVEOL is an AI agent automation company currently in pre-launch development. We collect information through this website for the purpose of managing our waitlist and communicating with prospective early partners.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address — when you join our waitlist or contact form
  • Name — if provided in our waitlist or contact form
  • Business type — if selected during waitlist registration
  • Message content — if you send us a message via the contact form

We do not use tracking cookies, third-party analytics, or advertising pixels at this time.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm your place on the AVEOL waitlist
  • To send you updates about our launch, early access, and pricing
  • To respond to your enquiries
  • To understand our audience and refine our product

We will never sell your data to third parties. We will never send you unrelated promotional content.

4. How We Store Your Data

During our pre-launch phase, waitlist data is stored securely. Once we integrate a formal email platform (e.g. Mailchimp, Beehiiv), your data will be transferred to that platform, which maintains its own privacy standards.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from any communications at any time
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@AVEOL.ai.

6. Third-Party Services

This website uses Google Fonts (loaded from Google's servers). Google may collect limited technical data as part of this service. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as AVEOL grows and integrates new tools. We'll notify waitlist members of any significant changes via email.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach us at hello@AVEOL.ai or via our contact page.
