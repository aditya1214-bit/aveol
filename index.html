// FAQ

Questions we
get asked most

What exactly is an AI agent?
A software tool that handles specific repetitive tasks in your business automatically — like responding to common customer questions, sorting emails, or generating reports.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
No. You just tell us how your business works. We handle everything technical.
How long does it take to get my AI agent?
After the consultation, most agents are ready within 5–7 business days.
What kinds of businesses is this for?
We specialize in operational and service-based businesses: marketing agencies, consultancies, real estate firms, and e-commerce brands.
What happens in the free consultation?
A 30-minute call where we learn about your business, identify your biggest time-wasting tasks, and tell you exactly what an AI agent could do for you. No commitment required.
How much does it cost?
Pricing is discussed after the consultation based on your exact requirements. Because every business is different, we provide a full, transparent custom quote before any work begins.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
We iterate until you're satisfied. We work closely with you during testing to ensure the agent executes its tasks exactly as you need it to before final handover.
Will the agent keep working after delivery?
Yes. We offer ongoing support, maintenance, and regular updates to ensure your AI agent adapts smoothly if your business workflows or software tools evolve.
